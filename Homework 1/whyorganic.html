     Some of the reasons why you should eat organic...
  • Get the nutrition you need and enjoy tastier food!
  • Keep chemicals from fertilizers and pesticides off your plate.
  • Reduce health risks associated with chemical fertilizers and pesticides.
  • No Genetically Modified Organisms (GMO) in your food.
  • Keep irradiation away from your food.
  • Prevent soil erosion & water pollution.
  • Protect future generations.
  • Protect farm workers and help small farmers.
  • Build healthy soil and an eco-system surrounding it.
  • Promote bio-divrsity.
